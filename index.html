What is EmojiMyFace and how does it work?

EmojiMyFace is an AI-powered face-to-emoji transformation tool that converts human faces in photos into matching emojis. Our advanced AI technology analyzes facial expressions, features, and emotions in your photos and replaces them with the most appropriate emojis while preserving the background. The entire process is quick, secure, and happens directly in your browser using Google's Gemini AI technology.

Is my data safe when using EmojiMyFace?

Yes! We process all photos locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What photo formats and sizes does EmojiMyFace support?

EmojiMyFace supports common image formats including JPEG, PNG, and WebP. The maximum file size is 10MB.

How accurate is EmojiMyFace at detecting faces and emotions?

Our AI is highly accurate at detecting faces and emotions in clear photos. For best results, use well-lit images where faces are clearly visible. The technology can recognize various expressions including happiness, sadness, anger, surprise, and more.

Can I process multiple faces in one photo with EmojiMyFace?

Yes! Our AI can detect and transform multiple faces in a single photo. The exact number depends on the clarity and visibility of faces in the image.

How can I use the emoji face images created with EmojiMyFace?

You can download your emojified photos and use them for social media profiles, sharing with friends, creating fun content for your brand, or any personal use. The transformed images are perfect for standing out on platforms like Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook.

Will EmojiMyFace work on my device?

EmojiMyFace works on most modern devices with updated browsers. We recommend using Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge for the best experience. The app is compatible with both desktop and mobile devices.
